Yes to Life

Do you know that in Australia alone, there are 3, 249 deaths by suicide in 2022 ? This based on Suicide Prevention Australia. It only reflects that there are still alot to be done. People need help and I think we can by using technology.

How about a social media app dedicated to those who are struggling in their life. People who can help others without being too affected or lets say have the emotional maturity can also register and provide FREE advice for those who are seeking help.

"There is no need for this, there are hotline provided by most companies." I hear you, but some people prefer to vent their thoughts on social media and not all are vocal. I am still unsure how to implement this but this is an idea that I think I can create soon.
